About Sermfy Blog

Practical guides and insights for church administrators across Africa.

Our mission

Sermfy was built for African churches — to make administration simpler, attendance tracking effortless, and financial management transparent. This blog exists to help you get the most out of Sermfy and church management in general.

We write about membership management, attendance tracking, SMS communication, finance, follow-up strategies, and everything in between. No fluff, just practical content you can use on Sunday.

The platform

Sermfy is a complete church management system that handles membership, events, attendance, tithes and offerings, bulk SMS, groups, follow-up, and reporting — all in one place. It works on any device, no app required.
